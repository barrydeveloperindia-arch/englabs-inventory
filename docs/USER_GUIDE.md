# 📱 Command OS - User Guide

Welcome to the **ENGLABS INVENTORY Command OS**. This guide will help you get started with the application.

## 🚀 Getting Started

### 1. Launching the App
- **Web:** Navigate to `https://hop-in-express-command-os.web.app` (or your local deployment URL).
- **Mobile:** Open the installed `ENGLABS INVENTORY` app on your Android device.

### 2. Logging In
1.  Enter your **Staff Email** (e.g., `gaurav@englabs.com`).
2.  Enter your **Password**.
3.  Click **"Access System"**.
    - *Note:* If this is the first time setting up the device, an Admin may need to click "Initialize Fleet" on the login screen (Debug Mode only).

## 🛠️ Key Features

### 📋 Dashboard (Staff Check-in)
- **Clock In:** Click your name or scan your barcode to clock in. Enter your PIN (`0000` default).
- **Status:** View who is currently working and their shift status.

### 👥 Team View (Org Chart)
- Click the **"Team"** tab.
- **Mobile:** You will see a vertical list of Management, Support, and Team members.
- **Desktop:** You will see a full organizational tree structure.
- **Contact:** Click on a staff card to view details (if authorized).

### 📅 Calendar & Rota
- Click the **"Calendar"** tab.
- View your upcoming shifts and shift history.
- **Managers:** Can access the **"Rota Planner"** tab to assign shifts via drag-and-drop.

### 🏝️ Leave Requests
- Click **"Request Leave"** or go to the **"Requests"** tab.
- Select dates and reason. Managers will approve/reject requests from their dashboard.

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

- **Forgot Password?** Contact a Manager to reset your account.
- **Wrong Role?** The system auto-assigns roles based on your email. Ensure you are using the correct `englabs.com` email.
- **App Issues:** If the app freezes, pull down to refresh (Mobile) or reload the page (Web).
