# ENGLABS INVENTORY - User Onboarding Video Script

**Duration:** Approx. 2 Minutes
**Target Audience:** New Managers and Store Owners
**Tone:** Professional, Efficient, and Empowering

---

## Scene 1: Introduction & Login
**Visual:** Show the "Access Terminal" Lock Screen.
**Action:** User enters PIN (e.g., 1-1-1-1) and clicks "Unlock".
**Voiceover:** 
"Welcome to ENGLABS INVENTORY Command OS. Security is our priority, so every session begins at the Access Terminal. Simply enter your personal PIN or use Face ID to securely unlock your customized management dashboard."

## Scene 2: The Command Center (Dashboard)
**Visual:** Dashboard loads. Highlight "Gross Sales", "Inventory Value", and charts.
**Action:** Mouse hovers over the Sales Trend chart.
**Voiceover:** 
"This is your Command Center. At a glance, you can see your real-time Gross Sales, total Inventory Value, and active Staff count. The interactive charts below give you immediate insight into your sales performance over the week."

## Scene 3: Inventory Management
**Visual:** Click "Inventory" in sidebar. detailed table loads.
**Action:** Scroll through the list. Click on a product to edit (or show the 'Edit' pencil).
**Voiceover:** 
"Managing stock is effortless. The Inventory module handles over 1,400 SKUs with ease. You can search, filter, and update stock levels instantly. Low-stock alerts will appear here automatically, ensuring you never miss a sales opportunity."

## Scene 4: Staff & Rota
**Visual:** Click "Staff Management" in sidebar. Show the "Team" or "Rota Planner" view.
**Action:** Drag and drop a shift or hover over a staff member's card.
**Voiceover:** 
"Your workforce is your greatest asset. In the Staff Management view, you can view your team's live attendance status synced perfectly to UK Standard Time, manage the weekly Rota with simple drag-and-drop actions, and even approve leave requests directly from the system."

## Scene 5: Financials & Reporting
**Visual:** Click "Financials" in sidebar.
**Action:** Briefly show the P&L or Ledger view.
**Voiceover:** 
"Complete financial transparency is just a click away. Track every transaction, expense, and purchase order to keep your business profitable and compliant."

## Scene 6: Conclusion
**Visual:** Click "Take a Tour" button, then "End Session".
**Voiceover:** 
"Need a refresher? Click 'Take a Tour' at any time for a guided walkthrough. ENGLABS INVENTORY Command OS—empowering your retail business. Thank you."
