# Command OS: Manager Handbook
**The new Command OS empowers you to manage the store efficiently, ensuring compliance, operational excellence, and staff satisfaction.**

---

## 🛡️ Compliance & Audit (Crucial!)

### Audit Trail
*   **Where**: "Compliance" Tab in Staff View.
*   **Why**: Every critical action—Stock updates, Shift changes, Cash refunds—is logged here.
*   **Action**: Review this daily.
    1.  Look for "High Severity" logs (marked Red).
    2.  Check for unusual "Stock Adjustments" (e.g., -5 items without explanation).
    3.  Export to Excel weekly for archival.

### Staff Performance Monitoring
*   **Lateness**: Check the "Attendance" tab for staff clock-in times.
*   **Task Completion**: Monitor the "Daily Checklist" for timely completions.
*   **Sales Voids**: High void rates per staff member might indicate training needs or theft.

## 👥 Workforce Management
![Smart Rota Planner](images/05_staff_rota.png)
### Planning the Rota
1.  Navigate to **Staff > Rota Planner**.
2.  Drag and drop staff onto shift slots.
3.  Check "Availability" indicators (Green = Available, Red = Busy).
4.  **Publish** the Rota only when finalized (Staff receive notifications).
5.  **Changes**: Edit shifts directly. Staff will be notified of updates.

### Approving Leave
1.  Check the **"Pending Requests"** section in the Dashboard.
2.  Review the dates against the Rota.
3.  Tap **Approve** or **Reject** (with a reason).
4.  The system automatically updates the Rota if approved.

## 📦 Inventory Control
![Inventory View](images/04_inventory_list.png)
### Stock Adjustments
*   **Correcting Counts**: Use the "Adjust Stock" button responsibly.
    *   *Always* add a reason (e.g., "Expired", "Damaged", "Supplier Issue").
*   **Expiry Management**: Check the "Expiry Risk" dashboard weekly.
    *   Apply "Reduce to Clear" labels for items nearing expiry.
*   **Pricing**: Update prices via the "Product Edit" screen. ensure shelf labels are reprinted.

---

## 📢 Communication
*   **Announcements**: Post messages to the staff dashboard (e.g., "Stock Take Sunday").
*   **Feedback**: Encourage staff to report bugs or suggestions via the "Help" module.

---

## 🚨 Emergency Procedures
*   **Terminal Lockout**: If a staff member is locked out, use your Manager PIN to verify their identity and reset access.
*   **Offline Mode**: The system works offline. Data syncs automatically once internet returns.
    *   *Note*: Ensure tills are reconnected promptly to avoid data conflicts.
