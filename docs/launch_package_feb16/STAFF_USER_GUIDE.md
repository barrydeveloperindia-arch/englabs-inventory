# Command OS: Staff User Guide
**Welcome to your new digital workspace!**

This guide provides everything you need to know about using the ENGLABS INVENTORY Command OS for your daily duties.

---

## 📅 Getting Started

### 1. Clocking In (Attendance)
![Login Screen](images/01_login_screen.png)
1.  Approach the **Access Terminal** on the shop floor or office.
2.  Tap on your profile picture or the **Biometric** icon.
3.  Enter your **4-Digit PIN** or use Face Recognition if enrolled.
4.  Select **Start / Unlock**.
5.  Your shift officially starts! The system logs your arrival time.

### 2. Your Dashboard
Once logged in, you'll see your personalized dashboard:
*   **Today's Tasks**: Checklists like "Morning Cleaning" or "Fridge Temperature Log".
*   **Inventory Alerts**: Items that need restocking or shelf edge labels.
*   **Messages**: Important announcements from management.

### 3. Smart Tasks
*   Tap on a task (e.g., "Clean Coffee Machine") to view instructions.
*   Complete the task and strictly adhere to the schedule.
*   Upload a photo if required (e.g., for "Spill clean-up").
*   Mark as "Done". The system timestamps your completion.

---

## 🛒 Using the Register & Inventory

### Scanning Items
1.  Navigate to **Sales View**.
2.  Scan item barcodes or search by name.
3.  Tap categories (e.g., Tobacco, Soft Drinks) for quick access.
4.  Ensure **Wait for Payment** before finalizing.

### Restocking & Shelf Labels
1.  Navigate to **Inventory**.
2.  Search for the product by name/SKU.
3.  Tap **Print Label** if the shelf price tag is missing or outdated.
4.  Update **Stock Quantity** only if you have physically counted the items.
    *   *Note: All stock changes are logged in the Audit Trail.*

---

## 🗓️ Your Rota & Leave

### Checking Your Rota
1.  Navigate to the **Staff Tab > Calendar**.
2.  View your upcoming shifts for the week/month.
3.  Use the filter to see only your shifts or the full team roster.

### Requesting Leave
1.  Go to **Attendance > Leave Request**.
2.  Select the dates and type (Annual Holiday / Sick Leave).
3.  Enter a reason (optional).
4.  Tap **Submit**.
5.  You will receive a notification once a Manager approves or rejects it.

---

## ⚠️ Troubleshooting
*   **Login Failed?**: Ensure you are using the correct PIN. After 3 failed attempts, ask a Manager to unlock the terminal.
*   **System Freeze?**: Wait 30 seconds. If still frozen, notify the Store Manager immediately.
*   **Mistake Logged?**: Don't worry. Inform your Manager so they can annotate the Audit Log. Do not try to delete unauthorized records.
