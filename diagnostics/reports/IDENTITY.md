# PROJECT IDENTITY: ENGLABS Inventory Management

## 🎯 VISION
A professional, high-fidelity Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) platform for deep-stack inventory, workforce, and project management, specifically calibrated for the Indian industrial context (Panchkula, Haryana).

## 🏛️ CORE PRINCIPLES
1. **Industrial Reliability**: The system must operate 24/7. Offline resilience is mandatory.
2. **High-Fidelity UI**: "Industrial Clean" aesthetics. High contrast, professional typography (Inter), and premium animations.
3. **Audit-Ready**: Every asset, every transaction, and every staff action must leave a cryptographically sound audit trail.
4. **Agentic Coordination**: The software is not just a tool; it is an autonomous manager.

## 👥 TARGET ROLES
- **Owner/Director**: High-level fiscal intelligence.
- **Manager/Coordinator**: Operational efficiency and team scheduling (Rotas).
- **Inventory Staff**: Real-time stock scans and procurement updates.
- **Shop Assistant**: Customer-facing transactions and POS logic.

## 📍 LOCAL CONTEXT
- **Region**: India (GST compliance, 24-hour time format).
- **Identity**: "ENGLABS" (Engineering labs for industrial excellence).
